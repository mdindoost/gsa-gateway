Student Initiatives

Have an idea to improve graduate student life at NJIT? We want to hear it.

Make your voice heard

GSA Gateway's initiative system lets any graduate student submit a proposal, idea, or request directly to GSA officers — anonymously if you choose. Whether it's a new workshop, a policy change, a social event, or a resource you wish existed, we want to know.

All submissions are reviewed by GSA officers and discussed at executive board meetings. High-impact ideas may be escalated to university administration with your permission.

Submit via Discord

Type /initiative in any GSA Discord channel. A private form will open. Your identity is anonymous unless you choose to share contact info.

Join the Discord Server →

Available Categories

academic

Courses, advising, academic support, library resources.

social

Social events, community building, cultural celebrations.

career

Career fairs, industry connections, internships, alumni.

wellness

Mental health, fitness, work-life balance, stress relief.

funding

Financial aid, travel grants, research funding, stipends.

international

Visa support, cultural inclusion, language resources.

research

Research infrastructure, computing, lab resources, symposia.

other

Anything that doesn't fit neatly into the above.

How it works

  1. Open Discord and go to any GSA channel.
  2. Type /initiative and press Enter.
  3. Fill in the private form: title, description, category, and optionally your contact info.
  4. Submit — your entry is logged securely and sent to GSA officers.
  5. Officers review submissions at the next executive board meeting.
