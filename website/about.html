About GSA Gateway

Connecting NJIT graduate students with the resources and representation they deserve.

What is GSA?

The Graduate Student Association (GSA) at NJIT is the official representative body for all graduate students at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. Every enrolled graduate student is automatically a member — no application required.

GSA advocates for graduate student interests at the university level, organizes academic and social events, connects students with campus resources, and gives graduate students a formal voice in university governance through faculty and administrative committee representation.

What is GSA Gateway?

GSA Gateway is an AI-assisted student communication initiative designed to make GSA information, events, resources, and student ideas more accessible to all NJIT graduate students.

It consists of two parts: a Discord bot that answers questions, lists events, collects anonymous feedback and initiative proposals, and a static website (this site) that mirrors event listings and resource information for students who prefer browsing the web.

The project is fully open source, runs on free infrastructure, and was built in response to graduate students expressing difficulty finding GSA information in one accessible place.

Privacy Commitment

GSA Gateway is designed with student privacy as a core principle. Your Discord user ID is never stored in plain text. All identifiers are hashed with SHA-256 before database storage. Contact information is only collected and stored when you explicitly opt in (for example, when you request a follow-up after submitting an initiative).

Anonymous feedback and questions help us understand what graduate students need without requiring anyone to put their name on it. We believe accessibility and privacy can coexist.
