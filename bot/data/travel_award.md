## Travel Award Program — Overview
The GSA Travel Award supports graduate students attending academic conferences, presenting research, or participating in professional development activities. Awards are granted on a rolling basis throughout the academic year, subject to available funds. Contact gsa-travel@njit.edu for questions.

## Maximum Award Amount
The maximum travel award is $900 per student per fiscal year (September 1 – August 31). Students may apply multiple times per year but cannot exceed $900 total in one fiscal year. Awards are not carried over to the next fiscal year. Partial awards are available if the $900 cap has been partially used.

## Eligibility Requirements
To be eligible for the GSA Travel Award, you must:
- Be a currently enrolled NJIT graduate student (full-time or part-time)
- Have a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0
- Be presenting a paper, poster, or research at the conference, or have documented academic justification
- Submit the application at least 3 weeks before the travel date
- Not have any outstanding reimbursements or financial holds with GSA or NJIT

## Chrome River Submission — Required Documents
When submitting your travel award reimbursement through Chrome River, you must include all of the following 7 documents:
1. **Completed GSA Travel Award Application Form** (signed by your faculty advisor)
2. **Conference acceptance letter or program listing** showing your name and presentation title
3. **Original flight, train, or bus receipts** (booking confirmations are not accepted; must show actual payment)
4. **Hotel receipts** (official invoice showing nightly rate, dates, and your name as the guest)
5. **Conference registration receipt** (proof of full payment of the registration fee)
6. **GSA approval email** (the confirmation email you received from gsa-travel@njit.edu after your application was reviewed)
7. **Completed W-9 form** (required for all reimbursements over $100; available at the GSA office)

Missing any of these 7 documents will result in your Chrome River submission being returned without processing. Submit at: myadmin.njit.edu/finance (Chrome River portal).

## Application Process
1. Complete the GSA Travel Award application form (available at Campus Center 110A or gsa.njit.edu)
2. Email the completed form to gsa-travel@njit.edu with subject line: "Travel Award Application – [Your Name]"
3. Receive an approval or denial email from the GSA Finance Committee within 5–10 business days
4. Travel and collect all required original receipts
5. Submit Chrome River reimbursement request within 30 days of your return date
6. Reimbursements are processed within 2–4 weeks of a complete Chrome River submission

## Covered Expenses
The travel award covers: conference registration fees, round-trip transportation (economy class only), hotel accommodations (up to $150 per night), and ground transportation such as taxis or rideshare with receipt. It does NOT cover: meals (unless meals are included in the conference registration fee), personal expenses, checked baggage beyond one bag, entertainment, or alcohol.

## Award Deadlines
Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. However, the fiscal year closes August 31; applications for travel occurring in August must be submitted no later than August 1 to allow processing time. Retroactive applications (submitted after travel) are not accepted under any circumstances.
