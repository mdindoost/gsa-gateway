# Maintaining Your F-1 Status

You are responsible for knowing and following these requirements to maintain your F-1 status. Keep copies of your own records, including all prior Form I-20s — OGI can only reprint your most recent I-20.

## Enroll full-time every required semester
Fall and Spring are mandatory; summer and winter are optional.
- **Graduate students must take at least 9 credits; undergraduates at least 12 credits** each fall and spring.
- Only **3 online credits** count toward full-time enrollment per semester (the rest must be in person — face-to-face, hybrid, or converged learning attended in person).
- In your **final semester** you may take only the coursework required to finish, but you must have **at least one in-person course** and still follow the online-credit limit.
- **Talk to your OGI adviser BEFORE dropping below full-time or taking a leave of absence** — exceptions are rare.
- During summer/winter you may take any number of courses (including all online, even from outside the U.S.), but you must return in person for the fall.

## Keep your documents current
- Update your **local address and personal information within 10 days** of a change, through Highlander Pipeline. Changes of name, major, or financial sponsor require a **new I-20** — contact OGI.
- Keep your **passport valid at all times** and your **I-20 valid**. If you need more time, apply for a **program extension BEFORE your I-20 end date**, or you will fall out of status. Your I-20 end date is shortened if you finish early.
- Your **F-1 visa may expire while you are in the U.S.** as long as your I-20 (or OPT EAD) is valid, but you need a valid visa to **re-enter** after traveling abroad. Visa renewal abroad is handled by the U.S. Embassy or Consulate — OGI does not advise on that process.

## Work rules
**Never work off-campus without prior authorization** (CPT or OPT). Working without authorization is a serious violation of your F-1 status and will **terminate your SEVIS record**. On-campus employment has its own rules (see that overview).

## Tell OGI about changes and travel
- **Notify OGI** if you change immigration status (for example to H-1B or permanent resident), leave the U.S. after graduation or OPT, or want a **Leave of Absence** — contact OGI **before** dropping courses or leaving.
- Discuss any **transfer** plans with an OGI adviser (they complete the SEVIS transfer).
- Request a **travel signature** before traveling outside the U.S.: it is valid **1 year** while studying and **6 months** while on OPT. You need a valid visa to re-enter.

## Confirm with OGI
When in doubt about your status, contact OGI before acting: https://www.njit.edu/global
