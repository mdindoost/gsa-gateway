# OGI Forms and I-20 Requests

The Office of Global Initiatives (OGI) handles F-1 and J-1 immigration forms and Form I-20 requests. Most requests are submitted electronically through the **OGI Application & Document Request form**; a few — OPT, 24-month STEM OPT, Travel Request, Transfer Out, and I-20 Reprint — are submitted in the **myGlobal International Student Portal**. Processing is **up to 7 business days**, in the order received, and OGI cannot expedite. Once processed, you receive a new **Form I-20 by email**; print it and sign it in ink.

## Requesting a Form I-20
Use the **Application for Form I-20** if you are returning from a leave of absence, adding a dependent, updating your financial information or major, or applying for an internship with an International Organization. Include the **Financial Statement Form** when applicable. For an I-20 **reprint** (for example, after updating OPT employment information), submit the request in the **myGlobal portal** instead.

## Financial documents
The **Financial Statement Form** is how you show you have sufficient funding to cover your expenses. It is required with I-20 applications that involve new or updated financial information, adding a dependent, a change of status, or reinstatement.

## Full-time status / reduced course load
If you need to drop below full-time, OGI has forms for a **Reduced Course Load** for **academic** reasons (an adviser form) or **medical** reasons (which require U.S.-based medical documentation). See the "Maintaining F-1 status" overview for the full-time enrollment rules.

## Program extension
Use the **F-1 Extension Form** to extend your program end date **before** your current I-20 expires (J-1 students use the DS-2019 extension form).

## Other common requests
- **Change of Status to F-1** (from another visa) and **Reinstatement** to F-1 status (you must meet with an OGI adviser first) — both also need the Financial Statement Form.
- **Leave of Absence** for undergraduate and master's students (doctoral students contact the Office of Graduate Studies).
- **Dependent DS-2019** (J-1), **Cancel an F-2 SEVIS record**, and an **Invitation Letter** template for visiting family.

## Confirm with OGI
Submit requests through the OGI forms page or the myGlobal portal, allow up to 7 business days, and confirm current requirements with OGI: https://www.njit.edu/global/forms
