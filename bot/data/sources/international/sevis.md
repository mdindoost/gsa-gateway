# SEVIS, the SEVIS Fee, and Transferring Your Record

**SEVIS** (the Student and Exchange Visitor Information System) is the federal database that tracks F-1 students. You have a SEVIS record and SEVIS ID number tied to your Form I-20.

## The I-901 SEVIS fee
You pay the **I-901 SEVIS fee** once, when you first obtain F-1 status for a new program. If you **transfer your SEVIS record** between U.S. schools, you keep the **same SEVIS record and ID number** and do **not** pay the I-901 SEVIS fee again.

## Transferring your SEVIS record out to another U.S. school
If you are maintaining valid F-1 status, you may transfer your SEVIS record from NJIT to another DHS-approved school. This keeps your same SEVIS record and ID and lets you remain in the U.S. while you wait for your new program to start.

**Important:** transferring your record **ends any NJIT employment** (on-campus, CPT, or OPT) on the release date — you may not keep working at or through NJIT after your record is released. **Do not submit a Transfer Out request unless you are 100% certain of your transfer school and release date; once processed, it cannot be canceled.**

Key points:
- Submit the **Transfer Out request through the myGlobal portal** with your new school's acceptance letter and proof of your program start date; processing is **7 business days** and cannot be expedited.
- Timing: after completing your NJIT program or OPT, request before the end of your **60-day grace period**; classes at the new school must begin within **5 months** of your last enrollment (or your EAD end date). Mid-semester transfers must keep attending full-time until the release date.
- It is **your responsibility to drop your NJIT courses** (in Highlander Pipeline or with the Registrar); OGI cannot drop them for you.
- Ph.D. students who have not yet received their degree must meet with the Office of Graduate Studies first.
- If you travel after your record is transferred, you must re-enter the U.S. on the **new school's I-20**, not NJIT's.

## Transferring in to NJIT
If you are a newly admitted student transferring from another U.S. school, OGI coordinates moving your existing SEVIS record to NJIT — contact OGI (and Admissions) to start.

## Confirm with OGI
Start any transfer through the myGlobal portal and confirm timing with OGI: https://www.njit.edu/global
