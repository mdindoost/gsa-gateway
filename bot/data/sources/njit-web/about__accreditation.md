# NJIT Accreditation Status

## Institutional Accreditation

New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT) is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education (MSCHE), 3624 Market Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104 (267-284-5000).

MSCHE is an institutional accrediting agency recognized by the U.S. Secretary of Education and the Council for Higher Education Accreditation.

In 2022, NJIT successfully completed its self-study and was re-accredited by MSCHE through 2030.


The next self-study evaluation is scheduled for the 2029–2030 academic year.
