# Office of the Registrar

Handles: course registration, add/drop, withdrawal from a course or the semester, registration holds, transcripts, enrollment and degree verification, transfer-credit evaluation, grades and GPA records, graduation processing, and the academic calendar.

Contact: registrar@njit.edu · 973-596-3000 · Mon–Fri 9:00 AM–5:00 PM · https://www.njit.edu/registrar/
