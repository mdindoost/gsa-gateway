Get Help 24/7
During business hours, please call the Center for Counseling and Psychological Services (C-CAPS) at (973) 596-3414 and state your need for immediate help.
Get Help 24/7
For 24-hour assistance call one of the following:
Psychological/Psychiatric Emergencies
NJIT Public Safety 911 (indicate if you're at NJIT) or  (973) 596-3111; they can contact C-CAPS staff after hours or provide hospital transport.
University Hospital Psychiatric Crisis hotline (973) 623-2323
National Suicide Prevention Lifeline 988
Crisis Text Line- Text HOME to 741-741
NJ Hopeline (855) 654-6735
Sexual assault or domestic violence (24/7)
NJIT Public Safety (973) 596-3111
University Hospital medical emergency room (973) 972-1325, 1324, 5123; provides emergency medical and counseling services
Sexual Assault (877) 733-CARE, 2273
Domestic Violence Helpline (800) 572-SAFE, 7233
LGBTQIA+ resources
The Trevor Project- is determined to end suicide among LBGTQ youth by providing resources and a nationwide, 24-hour hotline.  If you are considering suicide or need help, call 866-4-U-TREVOR (866-488-7386).
