Student focused interactive web surveys.
Alcohol and Cannabis eCHECKUP TO GO
NJIT has partnered with
eCheckUps To Go
which was developed by psychologists at San Diego State University, whose mission is to provide students with personalized, evidence-based online behavior intervention programs designed to empower students to make healthier and responsible choices.
As part of our comprehensive prevention program for students, NJIT invites you to complete the Alcohol eCHECKUP TO GO and Cannabis eCHECKUP TO GO
programs. These online modules will empower you to make well-informed decisions about issues that affect your college years and beyond.
Alcohol eCHECKUP TO GO
A brief, self-assessment that provides you with accurate, detailed and personalized feedback on your use of alcohol, and specific information and helpful resources in the NJIT community.
Cannabis eCHECKUP TO GO
A brief, self-assessment that provides you with accurate, detailed and personalized feedback on your use of marijuana, and specific information and helpful resources in the NJIT community.
