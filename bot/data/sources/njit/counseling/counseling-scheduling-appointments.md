Scheduling Appointments
We’re Here to Listen and Help
Appointments
Welcome to the Center for Counseling and Psychological Services (C-CAPS) in Campbell Room 205.
The first step for receiving services is to contact us to set up an initial consultation appointment. You can call us at (973) 596-3414 or email
counseling@njit.edu
.
If you call after hours or reach voicemail, please leave information on how to contact you via phone, and we will call you to set up an appointment.
NJIT has also partnered with Mantra Health to offer students free virtual mental health services. If you are interested in learning more, click here for more information
http://www.njit.edu/counseling/mantra-health
