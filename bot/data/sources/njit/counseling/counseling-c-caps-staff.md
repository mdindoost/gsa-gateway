Email Warning:
General informational communications from NJIT students via email are welcome. However, because of confidentiality concerns and other considerations, we do not provide counseling services electronically. Please note that because of the nature of email, confidentiality cannot be guaranteed. In addition, because of staff availability and technical systems, access and responses to your email communication may be delayed. To protect your privacy and/or receive a timely response, we recommend that you call or stop-by the C-CAPS to talk directly with staff or leave a message.
