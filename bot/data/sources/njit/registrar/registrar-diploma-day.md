Diploma Information
Graduation Information
Replacement Diplomas:
(For the current figure, see the live page: https://www.njit.edu/registrar/diploma-day)
Replacement diplomas will be mailed out by Parchment to the address provided payment has been processed.
Submit your request form and receipt of payment to the Office of the Registrar or mail to: Office of the Registrar Att: Maryann Sawka New Jersey Institute of Technology University Heights Newark, NJ 07102
Use the following form to complete your request:
Replacement Diploma
Starting with August 2023 graduates, all diplomas and graduate certificates will be mailed directly to the student.
Students will be emailed by NJIT when their diploma is awarded and their transcript is updated.
Three weeks later, students will be emailed by Parchment with instructions and mailing information.
Students will have 72 hours from the Parchment email to confirm or change their address (this is separate from your NJIT permanent address), then diplomas will be mailed.
Note anyone with an outstanding financial obligation
will not
receive the email from Parchment until it is resolved.
Diplomas prior to August 2023 are available in the Registrar's Office, located in the Student Mall
Note we need to see a photo ID (Student ID, License or Passport) to release the diploma
If you graduated prior to August 2023, and are unable to pick up your diploma yourself, please see two alternative options below:
You can request to have your diploma mailed:
Email
Sawka@NJIT.edu
with your name, NJIT Student ID number, and mailing address. Please note ​diplomas are sent USPS certified mail, requiring a signature.
You may send a representative to pick it up:
They will need a signed letter authorizing them to pick up your diploma for you (
sample letter
), a copy of your photo ID (Student ID, License or Passport), and your representatives own identification in order for us to release your diploma to them.
Per NJIT policy legal names will be used on diplomas and transcripts.  Any student with an outstanding financial obligation will not be permitted to access to grades, transcripts, or diplomas.
