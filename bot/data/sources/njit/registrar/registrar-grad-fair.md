What if I can’t attend the Graduation Fair?
1. If you are unable to attend, you may send an authorized representative on your behalf to pick up all of your items. Authorized representatives must have the following:
-Signed letter of permission allowing them to pick up your regalia, and any other items -
Release Form
-Your NJIT ID card, or a photocopy of your ID card
-Their NJIT or State-issued ID card
2. Cap and gowns can be picked up from the Bookstore starting on Monday, May 11th.
3. Upon request, you can receive your cap and gown by mail.
-Please note you will be responsible for the shipping charges
