F-1 International Students
As an F-1 student at NJIT it is your responsibility to ensure that you are complying with all U.S. federal regulations and requirements of your immigration status
.
We urge you to review the information provided on our website carefully, to ensure that you are always in compliance with federal requirements and regulation.
If you have any questions about these requirements, contact the Office of Global Initiatives to ensure that you receive the most accurate information available. Our website is updated regularly in order to provide you with the most up-to-date information available.
Please review the information provided regarding F-1 Student Status and requirements carefully.
