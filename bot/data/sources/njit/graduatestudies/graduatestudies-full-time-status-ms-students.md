Full Time Status of M.S. Students
Current Students
Full-time master’s students are enrolled in at least 9 credits per semester (fall and spring). Students in good academic standing who are completing all degree requirements may enroll in less than 9 credits in their last semester in the master’s program.
Master's students in good academic standing who are registered in 701B (3 credits of Master's Thesis) can be certified full time with less than 9 credits if they have successfully completed, or are completing in the current semester, all required coursework in the program.
If you are an international Master's degree student registered for less than 9 credits, please apply online for
full-time certification for that semester here
. A prior approval will be revoked if you make course registration changes, and you will need to apply again.
International students should contact the
Office of Global Initiatives
for further information regarding immigration matters.
