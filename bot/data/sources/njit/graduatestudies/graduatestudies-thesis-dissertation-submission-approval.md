final defense
announcement at least
3 weeks
prior to the defense date (requires 2nd review of thesis/dissertation document to have been processed by GSO)
Submit your
Embargo form
to
gso@njit.edu
at least two weeks before the second review of your dissertation document
and at least one month in advance of your defense date.
Once your defense announcement has been posted, you must then forward the announcement link to your department admin for distribution.
Your dissertation defense must be held on-campus at an NJIT location that must be included in your defense announcement. All committee members must be present during the defense. If some, not all, of the committee members cannot be present and would like to participate online, your dissertation advisor must inform Dr. Ziavras (
ziavras@njit.edu
) in advance of your defense.
