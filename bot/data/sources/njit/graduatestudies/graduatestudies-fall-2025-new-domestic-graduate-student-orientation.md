Fall 2025 New Domestic Graduate Student Orientation*
Welcome to NJIT! We are pleased you will be joining us this Fall!
The Fall 2025 New Domestic Graduate Student Orientation
(For the current figure, see the live page: https://www.njit.edu/graduatestudies/fall-2025-new-domestic-graduate-student-orientation)
NJIT Graduate Studies
and the
Graduate Student Association (GSA)
designed to get you off to a good start in your graduate school experience. The event provides information about the resources available to you at NJIT. You will have an opportunity to learn about the graduate education programs, processes for academic issues, recommendations for success, available graduate resources, and NJIT services.
If you are unable to attend in-person, we also offer the
self-guided online orientation
which will provide information about the resources available to you as a graduate student at NJIT, summarize graduate policies and procedures, and provide guidance for success in your studies.
Activate your NJIT UCID
to confirm attendance for this upcoming Fall. You will need a NJIT UCID in order to access the
self-guided online orientation
. Remember that your NJIT UCID is different from your Student ID Number.
We are here to help you succeed at NJIT!
*Please note: This is an orientation for domestic students only. Incoming international students have a separate
virtual orientation
hosted by the Office of Global Initiatives.
FAQs
How do I register for the Fall 2025 New Domestic Graduate Student Orientation?
(For the current figure, see the live page: https://www.njit.edu/graduatestudies/fall-2025-new-domestic-graduate-student-orientation)
click here to register
.
Where do I park?
Please park at the NJIT Parking Deck at 154 Summit Street (enter at Colden Street and Warren Street). For directions to the NJIT Campus, click
here
.
Where do I check in?
Check in at the Campus Center Atrium (1st Floor). Refer to the
Campus Map
.
What is the dress code?
This is a casual event but first impressions are important. Please dress in business casual attire.
Will food be served?
Yes, dinner will be served. If you have any dietary restrictions, please indicate it when you register.
Can I bring a family member or guest?
Yes, feel free to bring a guest. Each student is limited to one guest. Please indicate it when you register.
What if I am unable to attend?
You can view the self-guided online orientation at your convenience which will provide information about the resources available to you NJIT graduate students.
I am a new international graduate student. Can I also attend this?
No. Incoming international graduate students will have a separate
virtual orientation
, hosted by the Office of Global Initiatives, with similar information and a focus on the unique needs of international students.
Questions about financial aid
As a full-time or part-time matriculated domestic graduate student with an admissions application on file, you may be eligible for some forms of financial aid.  If you have not already done so, submit the
Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)
.  Be sure to enter NJIT's federal school code, 002621. If you would like to speak to an NJIT Financial Aid Counselor, please visit the
Student Financial Aid Service Contacts
page.
Questions about classes or scheduling
Please contact the
Office of the Registrar
if you have questions about class offerings or your schedule. Reach out to your Graduate Academic Advisor for questions on your curriculum.  If you do not know who your advisor is, please contact the
Graduate Studies Office
and we can assist you.
