Guidelines for Graduate Faculty at NJIT
CREDIT-BY-EXAM (CBE) for Master's Degree Programs
In limited situations, assessment of a student's prior learning may allow the transfer of up to six graduate credits in specific master-level programs. The dean of the college that houses the specific academic program oversees the process.
