Professional Science Master's degree programs
Programs of Study
Earn targeted science or technology industry understanding, and gain the tools to lead in today’s competitive world.  NJIT’s tracks for professionals in master’s degree programs provide the topical expertise in addition to training in management, communications and finance.
Professional Science Master's Cyber Defense Option
, new option for the NJIT MS in Cyber Security and Privacy
(Affiliated with the
Professional Science Master's National Office
)
The MS in Cyber Security and Privacy (CSP) creates a strong foundation and detailed technical knowledge in security, privacy, and cryptography applied to computer systems, networks, and web applications. Graduates will have broad expertise in all these areas, with an option to cover not only technical but also legal, policy, and ethical aspects of security and privacy. In addition to in-depth knowledge of security mechanisms, standards, and state-of-the-art capabilities, they will also be able to design new systems and infrastructure-level security solutions.
This program will require 36 credits of graduate coursework in Computer/Information Science courses, Professional Communications courses, and Management courses.  Program faculty include NJIT's renowned experts in information security and data protection research as well as top industry leaders from NJIT's vast network of alumni, corporate partners, and change leaders.
NJIT has been designated as a National Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense Education.
This program is available now.

Professional Science Master's Cell and Gene Therapy Sciences Option
, new option for the MS in Pharmaceutical Chemistry
(Affiliated with the
Professional Science Master's National Office
)
The mission of NJIT’s professional Biotechnology option in the M.S. Pharmaceutical Chemistry program is to prepare scientists and engineers for dynamic careers in biopharmaceutical industry. The program will focus on providing integrated coursework and training in current biotechnology industry practices.
This program will require 30 credits of graduate coursework in Biochemistry, Organic Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Engineering, Professional Communications, and Management.  Program faculty include NJIT’s renowned experts in chemistry as well as top industry leaders from NJIT's vast network of alumni, corporate partners, and change leaders.

Professional Science Master's Professional Management Option
, new option for the MS in Information Systems
(Affiliated with the
Professional Science Master's National Office
)
The MS in Information Systems (IS) is a STEM degree that prepares students to become business and systems analysts, data scientists, systems and application designers, and designers of user interfaces and user experience.
This 36-credit program will create leaders with strong communication and management skills in addition to solid technical knowledge in data analytics, and the analysis, design, and implementation of information systems.  This option is designed for working professionals or students who already have acquired some professional experience.

Professional Science Master's Professional Management Option
, new option for the MS in IT Administration and Security
(Affiliated with the
Professional Science Master's National Office
)
The MS in IT Administration and Security (MS ITAS) is a STEM degree that prepares students with extensive practical experience doing IT administration. Security will be a component of core courses in addition to a specific security course. Those who complete the program will be well prepared to play leadership roles as database, network, security and web services administrators, as enterprise application administrators, and as IT administration managers.
This 36-credit program will create leaders with strong communication and management skills in addition to solid technical knowledge in databases, networking, security of web services, and enterprise applications.  This option is designed for working professionals or students who already have acquired some professional experience.
