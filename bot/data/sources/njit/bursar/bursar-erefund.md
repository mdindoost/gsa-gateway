Touchnet eRefund
Procedure on how to setup your eRefund profile on touchnet
Office of the Bursar
The New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT) has transitioned to a new eRefunds Direct Deposit system through TouchNet.
To receive refunds quickly and securely, students must enroll in the new eRefunds system, even if Direct Deposit was previously established.
Refunds issued through Direct Deposit typically arrive up to 7 days faster than paper checks.
Who Needs to Enroll
Students
All NJIT students must enroll in the TouchNet eRefunds system to receive refunds electronically.
Authorized Users
Authorized users (such as parents, guardians, or sponsors) who have been granted access by a student must enroll in the TouchNet eRefunds system if they manage refund banking information. Authorized users can enter or update the bank account information where refunds should be deposited.
Important Notice
Even if Direct Deposit was previously set up, banking information must be entered again in the new TouchNet eRefunds system.
Students who do not enroll will receive refunds by paper check, which can delay delivery.
How Students Enroll in eRefunds
Log in to MyNJIT
Visit
www.njit.edu
and sign in to MyNJIT.
Open the Student Account and Payment Portal (TouchNet)
Under Student Services, select Bursar’s Office and open the Student Account and Payment Portal.
Set Up Direct Deposit
Select My Profile → Electronic Refunds and enter your bank account information.
How Authorized Users Enroll
Go to
www.njit.edu
and access the Student Account and Payment Portal (TouchNet) using your Authorized User login.
Select My Profile → Electronic Refunds.
Enter or update the bank account information where refunds should be deposited.
