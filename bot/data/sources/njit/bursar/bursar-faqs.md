Bursar FAQs
CONTACT INFORMATION
Where is the Office of the Bursar located on campus?
The Office of the Bursar is located in the Student Mall under the parking deck on
Summit St
.  You may access the office by using parking Lot 3.
How do I contact The Office of the Bursar?
If you have any billing questions, please visit our office in the Student Mall.  We can be reached via email (
bursar@njit.edu
) or call us at 973-596-2877.
What time does The Office of Bursar close?
The Office of the Bursar is open
Monday - Friday from 8:30am - 4:30pm
.  At the start of each semester, the office will be open some late nights to accommodate students.  Holiday and Summer schedule may affect hours of operation.
When can I pick up petty cash?
Petty cash is disbursed between 9:00am - 3:30pm, Monday - Friday.  Hours are subject to change during the Holidays.  Please make sure your
form
is complete with authorized signatures, as well as original receipts.
I have questions about my financial aid.
Financial Aid questions should be directed to the Financial Aid office. Contact information for the
Financial Aid Office
.

PETTY CASH
What are the Petty Cash Guidelines?
Petty Cash Hours: Monday – Friday, 9am-3:30 (Reduced Days & Hours during summer)
The person receiving petty cash cannot be the same as the approver
The person picking up the petty cash cannot be the same as the approver
Approver must be a supervisor at least one level up.
Online expenses over 60 days must be reimbursed via chrome river
All other expenses over 90 days must be reimbursed via chrome river
(For the current figure, see the live page: https://www.njit.edu/bursar/faqs)
Pick-up person is required to show school ID
All receipts and signatures must be originals
The Petty Cash form must be completed in its entirety.
Students get reimbursed for taxes; unless the expense is for food.
