Our Mission
Under the
Division of Student Affairs
, the mission of the Office of the Dean of Students (DOS) is to create opportunities to engage and support students, both in and outside of the classroom, through general advisement, advocacy, special programs, problem-solving, and implementation of the
Code of Student Conduct and Academic Integrity Policy
