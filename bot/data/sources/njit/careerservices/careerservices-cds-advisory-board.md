CDS Advisory Board
The mission of the NJIT Career Development Services (CDS) Advisory Board is to provide CDS leadership with broad-based advice and guidance on strategies and tactics.
About
We Support:
Students:
Exploring career options, gaining experience, and preparing for employment, service, or graduate study.
Alumni:
Refining career goals, strengthening job search skills, and pursuing meaningful employment.
Employers:
Connecting with qualified students, graduates, and alumni while building partnerships with NJIT.
Community:
Linking students and alumni to service and civic engagement opportunities.
Advisory Board Role
The CDS Advisory Board provides strategic guidance that helps align our programs with workforce trends, employer needs, and student success. Members are invited to serve based on their expertise and commitment to advancing NJIT’s mission.
Member Responsibilities
Board members are expected to:
Attend three annual meetings.
Offer input on strategic planning, talent development, and employer engagement.
Advise on business development and partnerships.
Participate in subcommittees as needed.
Recommend resources and opportunities that advance CDS objectives and student success.
Advisory Board Meetings
The board meets three times a year (January, April, and September) at NJIT. Subcommittees may meet as needed, often via virtual conferencing.
Advisory Board Composition
The board is composed of:
HR and recruiting professionals
Hiring managers
Alumni with industry experience
Representatives from professional associations, community-based organizations, and government agencies
NJIT faculty, staff, and students
Members serve staggered two-year terms and contribute through their expertise and guidance.
