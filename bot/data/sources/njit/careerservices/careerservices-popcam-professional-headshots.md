Popcam: Get a new professional headshot today!
Popcam at NJIT revolutionizes how headshots are captured, offering you an easy way to access automated headshot services. Whether you’re looking for a new LinkedIn Headshot, organizing club portraits, or other professional photo needs, Popcam makes the process simple, efficient, and impactful.
​
Professional Headshots Made Easy
Students can get professional headshots taken at the
Career Development Services’ office, Fenster Hall 400
, or at selected
CDS events
, including the Career Fair!
You can schedule an appointment for your headshot using popcam by selecting the “Professional Headshot” appointment type in Handshake.
Clear directions will be displayed on the Popcam screen to help you get started..
A
QR code
will lead you to a website for quick signup and to receive your headshots directly.
Get your Headshot in 4 Simple Steps
Step 1:
Book an appointment on Handshake and visit the CDS office
– OR –
Find us at select events offering Popcam services.
Step 2:
Scan your ID code (QR code) at the Popcam station.
If you don’t already have an account, create one at lumatic.app
Once logged in, click the IDcode to view your QR code
Step 3:
Smile for three quick shots and pick your favorite!
Want a redo? No problem — take more until you’re happy.
Step 4:
Check your inbox for an email notification with a link to view your head shot on the Lumatic website.
Interested in borrowing the headshot camera for an event on campus?  Contact Carolina Barba Granda at
carolina.barbagranda@njit.edu
at least 10 days prior to the event for consideration.
