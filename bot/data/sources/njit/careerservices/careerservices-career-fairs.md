Career Fairs
Career fairs are excellent events to participate in for meeting employers from corporate, government, and non-profit organizations. NJIT's career fairs are hosted during the Fall and Spring Semesters by Career Development Services. Our students/
alumni
get to meet
hiring employers in-person
allowing connections with recruiters.
