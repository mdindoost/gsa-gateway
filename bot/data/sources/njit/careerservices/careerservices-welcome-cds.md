Welcome to CDS
At NJIT, Career Development Services is dedicated to preparing students for successful and fulfilling careers. We help students develop valuable skills, gain real‑world experiences, and build connections with industry partners to support their growth from the classroom to the workplace and beyond.
About
Our Mission
Career Development Services empowers NJIT students by enhancing their skills, experiences, and connections needed for meaningful careers. We serve as a connector between campus, industry, and community to drive career readiness and lifelong success.
Our Pillars
Pillar 1: Preparing Students for Career
Success
We work with campus partners to deliver career education and professional development that build awareness of career options, decision‑making skills, confidence, and overall career readiness.
Pillar 2: Employer Connections and Engagement
We grow and diversify employer partnerships to ensure all students have access to opportunities aligned with their academic and career goals. We track engagement and hiring outcomes to strengthen connections and ensure measurable career success.
Pillar 3: Experiential Learning
We make hands‑on learning central to career preparation by offering internships, co‑ops, service learning, and other real‑world experiences that develop skills and lead to strong career outcomes.
Pillar 4: Community Investment
We partner with public sector organizations to create service projects that benefit the community, advance student professional growth, and support the university’s mission.
Alignment with NJIT’s Strategic Plan 2030
Career Development Services plays a key role in supporting NJIT’s Strategic Plan 2030 by helping students develop the skills and experiences needed to succeed in their careers. Our work advances the university’s goals to prepare graduates who thrive in a rapidly evolving, technology‑driven world. Learn more about the plan here:
https://www.njit.edu/strategicplan/
