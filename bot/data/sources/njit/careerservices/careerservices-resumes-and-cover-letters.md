Get Prepared
There is no “perfect” or universally correct resume format. The format you select should be tailored to the  specific job you are seeking and reflect your unique experiences. The following pages provide various  resume formats. Review them carefully to determine which format or combination of formats will best  present your qualifications.
Additionally, consider how your resume will be processed by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), which  many employers use to screen resumes before they reach a human recruiter. To enhance your resume’s  compatibility with ATS, use straightforward formatting, incorporate relevant keywords from the job  description, and avoid using images or intricate layouts.
NJIT provides a platform called NJIT Hiration.com, where you can create your resume in an ATS-friendly  manner. This platform allows you to compare your resume with job descriptions for a better match and also  offers the opportunity to receive a resume critique from one of the career advisors.
Remember, the primary purpose of a resume is to secure a job interview. It is the interview that ultimately  leads to a job offer.
