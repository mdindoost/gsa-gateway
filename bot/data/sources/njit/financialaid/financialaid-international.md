International
Apply for Aid
Although international students aren't eligible for federal aid, there are plenty of other opportunities for financial assistance. Check out the
scholarships for international students
—and don't forget about
campus employment
. You'll need to wait until you get to campus to apply for any jobs, but it's never too soon to start checking things out.
