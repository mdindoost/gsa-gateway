# GSA Office and Contact

The Graduate Student Association (GSA) office is located in **Campus Center 110A**, open
**weekdays from 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM**.

You can reach GSA by email at gsa@njit.edu, the President at gsa-pres@njit.edu, or the
Vice President for Academic Affairs at gsa-vpa@njit.edu. GSA is also on Instagram at
@njit.gsa. To find a specific officer, just ask — for example "who is the VP of Finance?"
— and the assistant will give you their name and email.
